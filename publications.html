course materials

Laboratory in Psychological Science

An open course intended to introduce students to project development, experimental design, analysis, and interpretation.

[GitHub]

Human Memory

An open course based on Mike Kahana's Foundations of Human Memory. Covers classic experimental findings and experimental models.

[GitHub]

Naturalistic Data Analysis

An online book that includes a collection of state-of-the-art approaches to analyzing naturalistic data.

[GitHub]

Introduction to Programming for Psychological Scientists

A self-directed open course intended to introduce students to fundamental computational tools used by modern psychological researchers.

[GitHub]

Lab tutorials

A collection of lab tutorials on a range of quantitative and computational topics.

[GitHub]

Methods in Neuroscience at Dartmouth (MIND)

A 10-day intensive summer course on cutting-edge computational methods.

[GitHub]

Storytelling with Data

Data science course aimed at a general audience (no pre-requisites).

[GitHub]

Computational Neuroscience

A short University of Pennsylvania summer course that provides an introduction to programming in MATLAB, single-neuron models, ion channel models, basic neural networks, and neural decoding.
